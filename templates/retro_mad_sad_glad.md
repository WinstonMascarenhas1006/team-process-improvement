# Retro format: Mad, Sad, Glad

Timebox: 45 minutes

1. Quiet write (5 min): everyone adds sticky notes or chat lines in three columns
2. Share (15 min): group similar items, no blame language
3. Vote (5 min): dot vote on what matters most
4. Action (10 min): pick exactly one change for the next sprint
5. Close (5 min): who owns the follow up and when we check it

Example action items that worked for us:

- Planning ends after 90 minutes even if backlog grooming is not perfect
- Impediments must have a name next to them within 24 hours
- Big stories get split before sprint planning

Record outcomes with:

```
python src/retro_board.py add 3 --glad "..." --sad "..." --mad "..." --action "..."
```
