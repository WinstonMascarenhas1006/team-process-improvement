# Methodology and proof levels

This repo is a **research backed comparison tool**, not a claim that we ran ING or Spotify sprints.

## Three proof levels (shown in the dashboard)

| Level | Badge | Meaning |
|-------|--------|---------|
| **Primary** | Green | Data you or your team produced (student case KPIs tied to sprint json in this repo). |
| **Verified public** | Blue | Numbers or quotes traceable to a linked publication (ING, Spotify, GDS). |
| **Illustrative ops** | Amber | Sprint rows and impediments shaped like real agile teams, aligned to the headline trend but **not** copied from internal Jira exports. |

## What is solid proof today

1. **Student case**  
   Sprint points in `data/cases/student-peer-review/case.json` match the story (62% then 100% completion). Templates and CLI logs live in this repo. Code delivery is on your GitHub: [control-engineering-peer-correction](https://github.com/WinstonMascarenhas1006/control-engineering-peer-correction).

2. **Industry cases**  
   Each KPI card links to a **source title and URL**. The Evidence panel shows the exact public claim we relied on. Use those links in interviews.

3. **Compare view**  
   Puts the same dimension (e.g. time to market) side by side so reviewers see you understand differences in context, not random numbers.

## What would make it stronger (optional next steps)

Honesty beats inflated claims. The dashboard states the proof level on every section.
